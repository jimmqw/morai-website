ChatGPT-5 vs Claude 3.7

The Ultimate AI Model Comparison — Two Giants, One Winner?

四月 7, 2026 · 8 min read · By Morai

Two AI giants dominate the market in 2026. Here's our comprehensive comparison to help you choose the right one for your needs.

Quick Comparison

FeatureChatGPT-5Claude 3.7
Context Window128K200K
CodingExcellentGreat
WritingVery GoodExcellent
Price$20/mo$20/mo
Image GenYes (DALL-E 4)No

ChatGPT-5 Pros & Cons

Pros:
  • Built-in DALL-E 4 image generation
  • Fast responses
  • Great plugin ecosystem
  • Largest model ecosystem
Cons:
  • Smaller context window (128K vs 200K)
  • Can be verbose
  • More prone to hallucination than Claude

Claude 3.7 Pros & Cons

Pros:
  • 200K context window
  • Exceptional writing quality
  • Less hallucination
  • Extended thinking mode for complex tasks
  • Claude Code integration for developers
Cons:
  • No native image generation
  • Smaller plugin ecosystem
  • Slower on extended thinking tasks

Our Verdict

ChatGPT-5 for all-purpose use, image generation, and the widest ecosystem of plugins and integrations.

Claude 3.7 for long-form writing, document analysis, complex coding tasks, and anyone who values accuracy over breadth.

Both at $20/month, the choice comes down to your primary use case. For most people, having both isn't a bad strategy.
